IMAGE OF THE DAY
 
Nasa posts an "Image of the Day" page every day. Here are 3 of my favourite images:
 
 

IXPE Measures Exploded Star Remains

  

Sun Rings in New Month with Strong Flare

According to NASA, this image was captured, "When a massive star collapsed in the Cassiopeia constellation, it generated a supernova explosion with some of the fastest shockwaves in the Milky Way."		   According to NASA, "The Sun released an X1 solar flare, captured by our Solar Dynamics Observatory (SDO) on Oct. 2, 2022."
     

Grinning Gourd Decorates Milky Way
 According to NASA, "While observing the outer region of the Milky Way galaxy, our Spitzer Telescope captured this infrared image of a cloud of gas and dust that looks like the hollowed-out pumpkins we see every Halloween."
